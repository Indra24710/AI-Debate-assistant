Built using Metaphor and Angular

This app is a little experiment with the Metaphor API, and it finds you helpful results both supporting and opposing the argument/query you enter in the input box. The "Against" section uses rule-based approach to find the negation of the query, so sometimes it might fail to fetch the desired results.

Tips for Querying

  • Use queries with a positive or negative polarity for better results.
  • Sample bad query: How will AI change the world
  • Sample good query: How will AI positively change the world.
